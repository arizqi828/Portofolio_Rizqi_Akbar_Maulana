🗺️ Mapping & Analysis of Fishing Data — KKP
Dec 2025 – Jun 2026 · ArcGIS · Python
Spatial distribution analysis of tuna catches in WPP-NRI 573 based on catch logbook data from Indonesia's Ministry of Marine Affairs and Fisheries (KKP).

Constructed water zone layers (EEZ, Archipelagic Waters, Territorial Waters) from official reference polylines
Classified catch points by maritime zone
Output: 10+ thematic maps per fishing gear type + catch composition graphs per species
🐟 Skipjack tuna dominates catch volume compared to madidihang, bigeye tuna, swordfish, and albacore


🦑 Squid Stock Assessment — WPP-NRI 573
Independent Project · R Studio · ggplot2 · tidyverse · minpack.lm
Stock status assessment of squid in WPP-573 (South Java Indian Ocean) using KKP logbook data from 2018–2024.

📉 Biomass collapsed from 2,000 tons (2018) to near zero in 2024–2025 — well below BMSY (1,162 tons)
⚠️ 5 out of 7 years of actual catches exceeded MSY (549 tons) — clear indication of overfishing
📈 Fishing effort surged 32× in 2023 (225,897 sea days) with no matching increase in production
✅ Schaefer model fit: R² = 0.916 | CPUE standardization using GLM Tweedie


🌡️ Thesis — Thermal Front Analysis & Skipjack Tuna Migration in WPP-NRI 573
Undergraduate Thesis · ArcGIS · Marine Copernicus SST Data
Analyzed the influence of thermal fronts on the migration patterns of skipjack tuna (Katsuwonus pelamis) in WPP-NRI 573.

🌊 Mapped thermal front formation and seasonal variation from satellite-derived SST data
🐠 Correlated front locations with CPUE and tuna movement patterns
📍 Identified potential fishing grounds to support sustainable fisheries management

🏔️ Coastal Geomorphology — Digital Surface Model (DSM) via Drone
Oct – Nov 2025 · Agisoft Metashape · ArcGIS · Excel
